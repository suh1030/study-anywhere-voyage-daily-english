1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information when you use Notch Up!:

  • Account Information: Your email address and, if you use Sign in with Apple or Google Sign In, the identity token provided by those services. We do not receive your Apple or Google password.
  • Learning Progress: Which daily lessons you have completed, which flashcards you have marked as mastered, and your overall progress through the curriculum.
  • Voice Recordings: When you use the Speak practice module, you may record your voice locally on your device. The current version uses these recordings for local playback and does not upload them for AI feedback.
  • AI Practice Submissions: When you request Conversation feedback or chat with the Polaris tutor, your text and the context needed to answer are sent to our servers and to an AI service provider. Conversation feedback uses Anthropic; Polaris may use Groq or OpenRouter. We do not intentionally save the submitted message text in our application database after the response is delivered.
  • Purchase Information: We use RevenueCat to manage in-app purchases. RevenueCat receives purchase receipts from Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not receive or store your payment card details.
  • Credits Balance: The number of AI feedback credits you hold is stored in our database.
  • Technical Data: Our hosting, authentication, purchase, and AI providers may process technical records such as IP address, device or browser information, timestamps, and request logs as needed to operate and secure their services.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and maintain your account
  • To sync your learning progress across devices and sessions
  • To deliver AI-generated feedback on your speaking practice
  • To process in-app purchases and maintain your credits balance
  • To diagnose bugs and improve app stability

We do not use your personal data for advertising, do not sell your data to third parties, and do not use your data to build advertising profiles.

3. Voice Recordings

The Speak module allows you to record yourself shadowing short English lines and practicing your own answer. These recordings are processed locally on your device for playback only. We do not upload, transmit, or store your voice recordings on our servers. You may delete recordings at any time by clearing the app's local data.

The app requests microphone permission solely for this recording feature. If you deny microphone permission, all other app features continue to function normally.

4. Third-Party Services

Notch Up! uses the following third-party services. Each service operates under its own terms and privacy policy:

  • Supabase (database and authentication) — supabase.com/privacy
  • Anthropic API (Conversation feedback) — anthropic.com/privacy
  • Groq and OpenRouter (Polaris AI tutor) — groq.com/privacy-policy and openrouter.ai/privacy
  • RevenueCat (in-app purchase management) — revenuecat.com/privacy
  • Apple Sign In / Google Sign In — authentication only; we receive only a user identifier and optional email.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your account data and learning progress are stored using Supabase. We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, including encrypted network connections and database access controls. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

We implement Row Level Security (RLS) in our database, meaning each user can only access their own data.

6. Data Retention

We retain account and learning-progress data while your account remains active and as reasonably necessary to provide the App, resolve disputes, prevent abuse, and comply with legal obligations. You may request account deletion using the contact details below. We will complete verified deletion requests within 30 days unless retention is required by law.

We do not intentionally save Conversation or Polaris message text in our application database after a response is delivered. AI and infrastructure providers may retain request logs under their own policies.
