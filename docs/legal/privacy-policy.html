Privacy Policy

SAV Daily English  |  Effective Date: March 26, 2026  |  Last Updated: March 26, 2026

SAV Daily English ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information when you use SAV Daily English:

  • Account Information: Your email address and, if you use Sign in with Apple or Google Sign In, the identity token provided by those services. We do not receive your Apple or Google password.
  • Learning Progress: Which daily lessons you have completed, which flashcards you have marked as mastered, and your overall progress through the curriculum.
  • Voice Recordings: When you use the Speak practice module, you may record your voice locally on your device. These recordings are stored only on your device and are never transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly submit them for AI feedback.
  • Speaking Practice Submissions: When you request AI feedback on your answer in the Conversation module, the text of your answer is sent to our servers and processed via the Anthropic API. We do not store your answer text after feedback is delivered.
  • Purchase Information: We use RevenueCat to manage in-app purchases. RevenueCat receives purchase receipts from Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not receive or store your payment card details.
  • Credits Balance: The number of AI feedback credits you hold is stored in our database.
  • Usage Data: Anonymized analytics such as app version, device type, and crash reports may be collected to help us improve the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and maintain your account
  • To sync your learning progress across devices and sessions
  • To deliver AI-generated feedback on your speaking practice
  • To process in-app purchases and maintain your credits balance
  • To diagnose bugs and improve app stability

We do not use your personal data for advertising, do not sell your data to third parties, and do not use your data to build advertising profiles.

3. Voice Recordings

The Speak module allows you to record yourself shadowing short English lines and practicing your own answer. These recordings are processed locally on your device for playback only. We do not upload, transmit, or store your voice recordings on our servers. You may delete recordings at any time by clearing the app's local data.

The app requests microphone permission solely for this recording feature. If you deny microphone permission, all other app features continue to function normally.

4. Third-Party Services

SAV Daily English integrates with the following third-party services. Each service operates under its own privacy policy:

  • Supabase (database and authentication) — supabase.com/privacy
  • Anthropic API (AI feedback on speaking practice) — anthropic.com/privacy. Your submitted answer text is sent to Anthropic for processing. Anthropic does not use your data to train its models by default.
  • RevenueCat (in-app purchase management) — revenuecat.com/privacy
  • Apple Sign In / Google Sign In — authentication only; we receive only a user identifier and optional email.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your account data and learning progress are stored on servers hosted by Supabase in the Asia-Pacific region. Data is encrypted in transit using TLS and at rest using AES-256.

We implement Row Level Security (RLS) in our database, meaning each user can only access their own data.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required by law to retain it longer.

Conversation feedback requests are not retained — they are processed and the submitted text is discarded after the response is delivered.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, contact us at savelyn.siao@gmail.com.

8. Children's Privacy

SAV Daily English is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
